ABOUT

PROTECTING THE GENTLE GIANTS

Despite an international ban of whaling in 1985, over 47,000 whales have been killed since. The most troubling part is most have been done directly by organized governments. Japan, Norway, and Russia have been large contributors to this problem. Our mission at Whale Defenders is to do bring these issues to light in front of national governments, and show clear violations of international law . Unfortunately, governments like Japan have vast amounts of money, legal help, and influence to bury wrongdoings; which is why we need all the support we can.

With your help, we can bring these issues to the U.N. and other wildlife influencers. All donations go directly to these efforts. All reveneu from t-shirts and merchandise sold goes directly to the whaledefenders mandate. This mandate is a document currently with support from four countries, thousands of political officials, and five-hundred-thousand supporters. We thank all our contributors for their support, and know that every one, is one step closer to a world free of whaling. One small donation can make a giant impact.
